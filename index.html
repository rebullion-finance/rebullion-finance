About Rebullion

Rebullion Finance is a tokenized asset fund designed to provide a balanced hedge across multiple financial environments. Each REB token represents ownership in a diversified portfolio of USDC, tokenized gold, and Ethereum.

When users purchase REB, capital is allocated across three vaults. USDC ensures liquidity, gold provides long-term protection, and Ethereum generates yield through staking.

The value of REB is derived from the Net Asset Value (NAV) of all vaults combined. As Ethereum produces yield and asset prices fluctuate, REB reflects the total value of the fund.

Every reserve is publicly verifiable, creating full transparency. Rebullion is built to function as a modern hedge fund — combining stability, growth, and passive income into a single on-chain asset.
