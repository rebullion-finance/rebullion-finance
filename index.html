About Rebullion

Rebullion Finance is a tokenized asset fund designed to combine the strengths of traditional finance with blockchain transparency. Each REB token represents ownership in a diversified portfolio of USDC, tokenized gold, and Ethereum.

When users purchase REB, funds are automatically allocated into three core vaults:

• USDC for liquidity and stability
• Gold for long-term hedging
• Ethereum for growth and staking yield

The Net Asset Value (NAV) is calculated from all vault holdings. As Ethereum generates yield and asset prices fluctuate, REB reflects the total value of the fund.

All reserves are transparently stored and publicly verifiable. Rebullion is designed to be a modern hedge fund — simplified, secure, and fully on-chain.
