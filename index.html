About Rebullion

Rebullion Finance is a tokenized asset fund engineered to bring institutional-grade portfolio management on-chain. Each REB token represents a proportional share of a diversified reserve composed of USDC, tokenized gold, and Ethereum.

When an investor purchases REB, their capital is programmatically allocated across three vaults — each serving a specific financial purpose within the ecosystem.

The USDC vault provides liquidity and stability, ensuring that the system remains resilient during volatility. The Gold vault introduces a traditional hedge, protecting against inflation and macroeconomic uncertainty. The Ethereum vault functions as the growth and yield engine, generating passive income through staking rewards.

The value of REB is derived from the Net Asset Value (NAV) of all underlying vaults combined. As Ethereum generates yield and market prices fluctuate, the NAV evolves dynamically, directly impacting the value of the token.

Every asset within Rebullion is transparently stored in publicly verifiable wallets, allowing users to independently confirm reserves in real-time. This eliminates the opacity commonly found in both traditional finance and many crypto platforms.

By combining stability, real-world asset exposure, and blockchain-native yield, Rebullion Finance creates a unified financial instrument designed to perform across market cycles — a true all-weather hedge for the modern investor.
