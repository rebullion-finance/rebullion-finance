About

Rebullion Finance is a tokenized, asset-backed fund designed to give investors diversified exposure to stability, growth, and yield—without sacrificing liquidity. When a user purchases REB, their capital is automatically allocated across three core asset classes: USDC for stability and liquidity, tokenized gold for long-term macro hedging and inflation protection, and Ethereum for growth and staking-based yield generation. Unlike traditional staking or locked DeFi positions, REB holders are never locked into a contract—their investment is represented by a liquid token that can be freely transferred, sold, or redeemed at any time. The value of REB is determined by its Net Asset Value (NAV), calculated as the total value of all assets held across the vaults divided by the circulating supply of tokens. As Ethereum generates yield and market prices shift, the NAV updates dynamically, allowing REB to reflect the real performance of the underlying portfolio. All assets are held in transparent, publicly verifiable wallets, ensuring that every REB token is backed by real reserves. Rebullion bridges traditional portfolio management with blockchain transparency, creating a modern, all-weather financial instrument built for long-term resilience
